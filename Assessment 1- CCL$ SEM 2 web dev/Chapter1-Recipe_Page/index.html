Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Creamy, garlicky Alfredo pasta with tender chicken. Quick enough for a weeknight, but still feels fancy.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (fettuccine or penne)
  • 1 chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cooking cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Optional: parsley, chilli flakes

Method

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Melt butter in a pan and cook chicken until golden and cooked through.
  4. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds (don’t burn it).
  5. Pour in cream, simmer gently for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Stir in parmesan until the sauce becomes smooth.
  7. Add pasta to the sauce and toss to coat.
  8. Finish with pepper and optional parsley/chilli flakes. Serve.
