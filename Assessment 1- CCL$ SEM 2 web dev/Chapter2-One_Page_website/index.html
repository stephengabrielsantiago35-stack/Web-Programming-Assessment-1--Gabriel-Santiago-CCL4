Plot Overview

In a small town, a boy goes missing and strange events begin to ripple through everyone's lives. A group of friends investigates, uncovering secret experiments, frightening creatures, and a parallel world. The story mixes friendship, mystery, and sci-fi horror with an 80s vibe.

Key Information

  • Genre: Sci-fi, thriller, horror, drama
  • Setting: Hawkins, Indiana (1980s)
  • Themes: Friendship, courage, secrecy, growing up
  • Format: TV series

  • Lucas: Practical, cautious, brave when it counts.
  • Joyce: A mother who refuses to give up.

Reviews (Summary)

Many viewers praise the show for its suspense, characters, and nostalgic style. Some critics highlight its strong first season mystery and memorable monster-story tension. Others say later seasons focus more on action and scale, but the heart of the series stays the friendships.

Common compliment: "Great atmosphere and a bingeable mystery."

Common critique: "Sometimes the story gets bigger than the small-town vibe."
