Main Characters

  • Eleven (Jane Hopper): A girl with psychokinetic and telepathic abilities, escaped from Hawkins Lab.
  • Mike Wheeler: Loyal leader of the friend group, fiercely protective of Eleven.
  • Dustin Henderson: Clever and witty, often the source of comic relief and scientific curiosity.
  • Lucas Sinclair: Practical and brave, initially skeptical but always stands by his friends.
  • Will Byers: The boy who vanished; his connection to the Upside Down shapes the story.
  • Joyce Byers: Will's determined mother who never stops believing he's alive.
  • Jim Hopper: Hawkins' police chief, a gruff but caring guardian to Eleven.
  • Max Mayfield: A skilled skateboarder who joins the group; tough and independent.
  • Steve Harrington: Starts as a typical jock but evolves into a beloved "babysitter" and friend.
  • Robin Buckley: Quick‑witted and smart, works with Steve at the ice cream shop.
  • Nancy Wheeler: Mike's sister, a determined journalist who fights to uncover the truth.
  • Jonathan Byers: Will's older brother, quiet and artistic, always looking out for his family.
