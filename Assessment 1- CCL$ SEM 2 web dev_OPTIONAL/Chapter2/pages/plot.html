Plot Overview

In the small town of Hawkins, Indiana, a young boy named Will Byers vanishes into thin air. His friends – Mike, Dustin, and Lucas – stumble upon a mysterious girl with supernatural powers and a shaved head. As they search for Will, they uncover a secret government lab, a terrifying creature from an alternate dimension called the Upside Down, and a conspiracy that threatens their entire town. The series blends 1980s nostalgia, coming‑of‑age drama, and sci‑fi horror into an unforgettable story of friendship and bravery.
