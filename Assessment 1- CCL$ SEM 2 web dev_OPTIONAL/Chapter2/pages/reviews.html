Critical Reception

Stranger Things has been widely praised for its character development, atmosphere, and homage to 1980s films. The first season holds a 97% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes, with the consensus calling it "exciting, heartbreaking, and sometimes scary."

Common compliment: "A love letter to the '80s that works on its own terms – great kids, genuine scares, and a lot of heart."

Common critique: "Later seasons sometimes struggle with pacing and an ever‑expanding mythology, but the core cast keeps it grounded."

Audience reaction: Fans adore the characters' growth, the retro soundtrack, and the balance between horror and humour.
